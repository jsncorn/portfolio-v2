About Me

Born in August of 2003, I'm still considered a fresh, young mind. Despite being what others consider young, I have had a considerable amount of experiences in work environments. However, my experiences are not the only qualities I own.
I began coding in 2016 (yes, I know, I was super young). The reason I was so interested in coding was because of a video game I played. Because of this, I attempted to make a mod for said video game (written in Java). Through this sudden interest, I scoured the internet and self-taught myself on enough Java to finish my mini coding project. Safe to say, I was addicted to both the game and the code. Eventually, I took a break from coding as I did not have a guide and ended up not know what else to learn about.
In 2017, I began my coding passion again. This time, it would not be self-taught. My school provided a small pool of computer science classes. I began my first class in my sophomore year: Intro to Java. My prior experience helped me pass this class with breeze. So what next? I took another class - two classes actually. I ended up taking AP Computer Science Principles and Intro to Python. While Python was similar to Java, it would be a new language that I rather enjoyed learning and experimenting with. I ended up (independently) making a Python project that grabbed faculty member's info using user input. That is viewable here.
2020 began a new life experience for me: working with people in a working environment. I began work at in August of 2020 as a simple shop assistant. In a month, I was moved into a position as a shipping clerk. At this time, I was brand new in this area. With no previous experience with shipping items, I was determined to do my best. Within quite literally an hour or two, I had learned my entire way around my job: grabbing items off the shelf, creating shipping labels using a brand new software called Sage, packaging the items, then sending it out.
Towards the end of my time with , I was also asked to help with another portion of the shop. This portion was also completely unknown to me. I had never done machining in my life and never brought a thought to it previously. That did not stop me though. I learned what to do, why it does it, and how to do it efficiently in a quick manner. This job is where my strongest abilities are outlined. Though I may not know much, I am determined to learn and therefore very fast at learning.
